Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions? We have answers!

What are your opening hours?

We open from 8am to 10pm - 7 days a week.


What service do you offer?

We are currently offering Self-service, Service Wash, Ironing, Dry-cleaning.


Do you do the dry-cleaning in the shop?

No. We work with our high end partners to ensure that your garments are taken care of by the most experienced and specialised professionals.


Do I need to separate colored from white clothes?

Yes, please! It is customer’s responsibility to separate all items into bags for washing. If whites are not separate from colours and left in for wash where colours run, we will not be responsible.


If I am Allergic to some types of detergents, do I need to bring my own?

Your well-being is important to us, so you can request to use non-bio washing powder at no additional cost.


What temperature do you wash the clothes?

We wash all the clothes at a standard temperature of 40ºC, a different temperature can be used for delicate wash, white or heavy duty items. Just let us know your preference and we will follow your instructions.


Do you offer special care service?

For some fabrics special care is needed and cannot be done in the standard wash and dry service. Make sure to check the care label and separate it from your normal wash. When advised, our staff will take care of it and proceed with the recommended washing or drying process.


If I notice something is missing after a wash or dry service, what should I do?

Call us immediately, we will do all we can to locate your item. Although all the services and tagging are done individually for each customer, mistakes can happen.
